Welcome to my personal website! I’m an associate scientist in RAL at NCAR working with the WRF-Hydro team.

In general, my interests and experiences are in:

  • Operational hydrologic forecasting
  • Machine Learning application in hydrology
  • Hydrological Data Assimilation
  • Spatial/Temporal Downscaling methods
  • Geospatial Data Analyses
  • Remote Sensing
  • Uncertainty Quantification
